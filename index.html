Frequently Asked Questions

How do investment plans work? +

Our investment plans are designed to provide guaranteed returns along with additional benefits like grocery vouchers. You invest a fixed amount and receive monthly benefits plus annual returns.

When will I receive my grocery benefits? +

Grocery benefits are credited to your account monthly, starting from the first month after your investment. You'll receive a notification when benefits are credited.

Can I withdraw my investment early? +

Yes, you can withdraw your investment before maturity, but early withdrawal charges may apply. The charges vary based on the plan and withdrawal timing.

Is my investment secure? +

Absolutely! Your investments are backed by government securities and regulated by financial authorities. We use bank-level security measures to protect your funds.

How can I track my investment performance? +

You can track your investment performance through the dashboard in the app. It shows your total investment, active plans, and transaction history with real-time updates.
